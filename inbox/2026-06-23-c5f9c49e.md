**Document updated:** Q1 Retro + Q2 Planning

# Q1 FY27 SME Payments OB Retro Summary

The SME Payments team tracked performance across activation, onboarding speed, and compliance metrics in Q1 FY27, with mixed results: M0 activation improved to 18% (tracking toward 21% target), but signup-to-activation time increased to 37.3 hours instead of the 28-hour Q1 target, and 1-Click Onboarding achieved 0% adoption against a 5% Q1 goal. Key blockers include underperformance on organic M0 L2 submission (23.8% vs. 28.9% target) and stalled Re-KYC completion (0.65% vs. 10% target), requiring investigation into friction points in the L2 submission and verification workflows for Q2 planning.
