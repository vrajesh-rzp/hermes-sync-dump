**Document updated:** Onboarding Experience Strategy

# FY27 Integrated Strategy: Payments SME Onboarding

Razorpay's onboarding experience is losing merchants due to friction despite recent gains in conversion metrics (M0 Signup-to-L2 improved to 28.6% by Dec'25, though organic traffic conversion lags). The FY27 strategy focuses on four pillars centered on making onboarding "boringly predictable" internally and "refreshingly quick" for merchants, with a primary goal of achieving 40% Signup-to-L2 Submit rate to support the broader Emerging Business Net Revenue growth target from ₹30 Cr to ₹50 Cr. Key blockers identified include inadequate UX prioritization, observability gaps, platform robustness issues, and lack of merchant-type-specific proactive messaging, with execution details and P&L implications outlined in the full strategy document.
